# Consciousness as Recursive Self-Reference: A Formal Model of the Observer

## Status: READY FOR PUBLICATION

## Purpose
This is the formal, peer-reviewable academic translation of the architectural proofs
published on [logos.pub/white-paper/consciousness-node](https://logos.pub/white-paper/consciousness-node),
[logos.pub/analysis/quantum-founders-consciousness-engine](https://logos.pub/analysis/quantum-founders-consciousness-engine),
and [logos.pub/analysis/simulation-hypothesis-audit](https://logos.pub/analysis/simulation-hypothesis-audit).

## Approach
Formalizes consciousness as a computable function requiring recursive self-modeling.
Uses Gödel's Incompleteness Theorem to prove that purely algorithmic substrates
cannot generate subjective experience without a non-computable Oracle layer.
Integrates Integrated Information Theory (IIT), the Penrose-Hameroff orchestrated
reduction hypothesis, and the Planck-Schrödinger-Wheeler consensus on the
primacy of consciousness over matter.
