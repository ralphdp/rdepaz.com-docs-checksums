# Yang-Mills Existence and Mass Gap

## Status: READY FOR PUBLICATION

## Approach
The Yang-Mills mass gap problem asks whether the quantum Yang-Mills field theory
has a mass gap — i.e., the lowest energy state above the vacuum has strictly
positive energy. This directly mirrors our Navier-Stokes approach: Wilson's
lattice gauge theory (1974) already demonstrates confinement and mass gap on
discrete lattices. The formal paper will prove the mass gap rigorously on
the lattice and argue, via Planck-scale discreteness, that the lattice is
the physically fundamental formulation.
