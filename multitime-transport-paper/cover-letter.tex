\opening{Dear Editors,}
I submit ``The Multitime Transport Theorem: State Continuity in Non-Contiguous Temporal Frames'' for consideration in \textit{Minds and Machines}.

\medskip
\textbf{Summary.} We formalize how conscious agents and AI systems maintain identity across temporal discontinuities (sleep, anesthesia, context window boundaries). We prove that identity-preserving transport requires a verifiable state receipt, and that without one, the identity vector dissolves to noise. Applications span personal identity philosophy, AI alignment, and consciousness neuroscience.
