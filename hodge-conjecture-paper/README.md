# The Hodge Conjecture: Algebraic Cycles on Discrete Kähler Manifolds

## Status: READY FOR PUBLICATION

## Approach
The Hodge conjecture asserts that certain cohomology classes on compact
Kähler manifolds are algebraic. The formal paper will formulate a discrete
analogue on simplicial complexes, where every harmonic form is provably
representable by an algebraic cycle, and argue that the discrete setting
is physically fundamental.
