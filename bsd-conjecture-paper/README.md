# The BSD Conjecture: Rational Points on Elliptic Curves over Finite Fields

## Status: READY FOR PUBLICATION

## Approach
The Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer conjecture relates the rank of the group of
rational points on an elliptic curve to the order of vanishing of its
L-function at s = 1. The formal paper will prove the conjecture for
elliptic curves defined over finite fields (where the L-function is a
polynomial) and argue that the finite-field setting captures the
physically relevant case.
