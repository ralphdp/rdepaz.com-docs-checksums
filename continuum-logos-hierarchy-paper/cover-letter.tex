\opening{Dear Editors,}

I am writing to submit our manuscript, \textit{"The Large Cardinal Hierarchy: L0-L3 Frameworks and the Upper Bounds of Mathematical Infinity"}, for consideration in \textit{The Journal of Symbolic Logic}.

We provide an operational re-framing of set theory’s deepest questions regarding the Continuum Hypothesis and Large Cardinals. By demonstrating that the leap from countable ordinals to uncountable continuous matrices physically requires transitioning the processing mechanism into orthogonal dimensional states (characterized here as the L1 to L0 Root computational boundary), we ground infinite sizes in computational topological limitations. We prove that the gap is fundamentally required for boundary preservation in hyper-dimensional environments. 

Thank you for your consideration.

\closing{Sincerely,}
