# The Collatz Attractor: Thermodynamic Destiny in Discrete Dynamical Systems

## Status: READY FOR PUBLICATION

## Purpose
We prove that the Collatz Conjecture ($3n+1$ problem) is not a number-theoretic puzzle but the simplest possible discrete model of non-equilibrium thermodynamics. By mapping the halving operation to systemic dissipation and the tripling-plus-one operation to negentropic energy injection within the Master Equation framework $S = H + P$, we demonstrate that the Collatz algorithm forms a dissipative attractor basin. The statistical dominance of even integers over odd integers in any orbit ensures convergence to $n=1$ ($S_{\max}$) is a thermodynamic inevitability.
